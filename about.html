About Wellcare Orthopedic & Trauma Centre

Delivering expert orthopedic and trauma care with compassion, innovation, and excellence.

Our Story

Wellcare Orthopedic & Trauma Centre was established to provide specialized musculoskeletal care to the people of Kiambu and surrounding regions.


We are committed to helping patients regain mobility, recover from injuries, and improve their quality of life through modern orthopedic treatment and personalized care.


Our facility combines experienced specialists, advanced diagnostics, and patient-centered treatment to ensure the best outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and world-class orthopedic and trauma care that restores mobility and improves lives.

Our Vision

To become the leading orthopedic and trauma healthcare provider in Kenya through excellence in patient care and innovation.
